This dataset contains a total of 63,558 structures belonging to 6 different enzyme
classifications according to the Enzyme Commission (EC) scheme. More specifically,
these structures belong to EC classifications 1 through 6. Structures within this
set are composed of one or more protein chains. Statistics about
sequence length and structure diameter can be found in graph.summary.
